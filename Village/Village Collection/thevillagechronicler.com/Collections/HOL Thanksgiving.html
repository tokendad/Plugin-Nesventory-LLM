Thanksgiving / Fall
Revised 1/16/2021

Occasionally, throughout its history, Department has introduced specific holiday inspired designs that go beyond Christmas. This listing contains items designed with emphasis on Fall, Thanksgiving, and All Hallows' Eve. For collectors who like to display themed items for specific holidays, seasons, and special occasions those pieces are noted here. In as much as Snow Village Halloween and Legends Of Sleepy Hallow-een are specific village collections and Harvest Fields is a separate accessory collection, they are not a part of this listing.

This listing references the building along with its Coordinating Accessories (if so designed). Some items may not have a coordinating building or accessory. All of them reference the specific collection for which they were designed and where they may be found. Accessories contained in specific gift sets are not noted separately.
