Clock Shops
Revised 1/15/2021

Throughout Bavaria, handmade clocks are created through many generations of horologists (clock makers). The unique and intricate craftsmanship of each make them highly sought after and collectible. The same is true with regards to the village buildings that have inspired the designs created by the Department 56 artists.

The original ones were designed by Department 56 Master Architect Neilan Lund. Latter ones were designed by Barb Lund and Tom Bates. Due to the high collectability, as stated above, the buildings and any coordinating accessories are listed here.
