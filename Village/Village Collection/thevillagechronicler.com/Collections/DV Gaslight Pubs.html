Gaslight Pubs
Revised 11/4/2023

While pubs, also known as public houses, have played a pivotal role in the history of Dickens' Village, Gaslight Pubs is a specific subseries that was introduced into Dickens' Village in 2011. Flying Horse Tavern (#805518) was grandfathered in when the series began.
