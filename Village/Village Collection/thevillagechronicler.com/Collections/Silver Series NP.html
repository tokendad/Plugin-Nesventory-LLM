Silver Series- North Pole
Revised 6/10/2020

The Silver Series is a new classification that was introduced by Department 56 in 2018.

Releases, to date, have been in sets of four that include a building, accessory, Fresh Fallen Snow and a Sisal Tree. The buildings are designed by Paul Lundburg, North Pole Village Artist, in the same style as the porcelain collection for which it is named. While the Silver Series is considered a stand-alone collection, designs are easily incorporated by North Pole collectors. During 2018?, Department 56 did release three ornaments based off of the original three North Pole building designs.
