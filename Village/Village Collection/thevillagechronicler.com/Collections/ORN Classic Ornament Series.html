Classic Ornament Series
Revised 6/17/2020

The Classic Ornament Series was released twice. Originally, there were nine ornaments announced during the 1997 Mid-Year that did not have Pig-Tail Adapter lighting. They were retired at the end of 1997 and re-released under separate item numbers with the latter attached. Each ornament is made of ceramic or porcelain and comes in its' own individual house box with original artwork graphics.
