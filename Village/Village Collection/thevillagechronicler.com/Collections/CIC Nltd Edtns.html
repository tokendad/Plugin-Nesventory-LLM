Christmas In The City- Numbered Limited Editions
Revised 10/11/2024

Christmas In The City, like other village collections, has had numbered limited editions introduced over its long run. To date, there has been no numbered limited edition accessory for Christmas In The City. There have been several Coordinating Accessories that were not numbered; yet, designed to coordinate with a specific building that was.
