Brite Lites
Revised 1/19/2024

The Brite Lites accessories were created with the early LED technology of the 1990s. They were designed to enhance a village display by creating a more vibrant lighting effect. Most can be mounted on top of houses and buildings. Each Brite Lites accessory uses the Brite Lites Adapter.
