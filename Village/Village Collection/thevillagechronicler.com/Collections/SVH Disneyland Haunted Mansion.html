Disneyland Haunted Mansions
Revised 9/26/2025

The long-standing licensing agreement with Disney and the highly successful Snow Village Halloween VILLAGE made for an ideal agreement to add the Disneyland Haunted Mansion to the collection in 2021. The original building was introduced composed of ceramic. After production issues with the ceramic version, production ceased and the original concept of a resin building was instituted. In 2024, the Disney World Haunted Mansion was added as a First Edition and General Release.

While the Disneyland Haunted Mansion Gate and Disneyland Haunted Hearse designs are unique to the California location and Silas Crump & His Old Dog, Bones at Disney World, all other coordinating accessories may be found at both.
