Lighthouses
Revised 8/21/2019

Lighthouses prominently dot the coastline of the eastern coast and have proven to be very popular amongst New England Village collectors since they were first introduced in 1994. The Coastal Lights subseries was added in 2011. All lighthouses are included in this listing with the subseries specifically denoted here and is listed as a specific subseries in the general listing.
