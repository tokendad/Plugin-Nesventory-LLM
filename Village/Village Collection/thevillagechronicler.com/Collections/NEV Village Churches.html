New England Village Churches
Revised 8/21/2020

More often than not, churches are one of the first purchases a collector makes or receives as a gift. Several different churches, representing unique period architecture and coloring, have been designed for the New England Village. Due to the limited number of accessory offerings for them, some collectors incorporate the Christmas Bazaar accessories, horse drawn wagons, and village signs. It is not uncommon to see clergy from other collections used to accent the New England Village churches, as well. Though not a church, Mt. Gibb Parsonage and the Coordinating Accessories are included in this listing.
