ALPINE VILLAGE- Numbered Limited Editions
Revised 3/16/2020

Alpine Village, like other village collections, has had numbered limited editions introduced over its run. Granted, due to its issue size there have been a fewer number. Each are meticulously designed and encompass a variety of building designs synonymous with the Bavarian hamlets from which the collection is inspired. The Coordinating Accessories were not numbered or limited and the accessory contained in the boxed set was not named by the company.
