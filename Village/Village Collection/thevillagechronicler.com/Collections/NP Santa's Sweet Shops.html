Santa's Sweet Shops
Revised 11/1/2024

The North Pole and Christmas would not be complete without all of the delectable treats of the season. Each building is designed with unique features relevant to its type of business. Coordinating Accessories for each add to the charm of this uniqe subseries. They are great as a specific part of town in a display or tucked away and scattered between the other buildings designed for North Pole.
