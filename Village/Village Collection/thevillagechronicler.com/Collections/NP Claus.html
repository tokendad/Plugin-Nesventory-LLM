C-L-A-U-S
Revised 6/10/2019

As a continuation of the 15th Anniversary recognition of the North Pole series, each building features an elf proprietors' first name that spelled out “CLAUS”. The buildings also are accentuated with a custom gold initial weathervane that when displayed together, spell out “CLAUS”. When displayed beside their previously released companion buildings, they spell out “SANTA CLAUS”. Each building has its own Coordinating Accessories without special features.
