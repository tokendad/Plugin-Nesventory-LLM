Game of Thrones
Revised 1/13/2023

The Game of Thrones collection is part of the Hot Properties! Classification. It was introduced and well received with the Mid-Year introductions in 2021. Inspired by a series of books written by George R. R. Martin's “A Song of Fire and Ice”, Game of Thrones is the popular television drama based on the literary series.

The buildings are large, detailed façades with historical information regarding their place in the series on the back. They were designed by Department 56 Artist Tom Bates. The equally, detailed accessories are made of resin and designed by Accessory Artist Tate Yotter.
