Accents: Terry Redlin
Revised 6/17/2020

The Accents line of lighted buildings was introduced through the giftware division of the company. While designed by Village artists, this was an avenue to reach new customers through a lower price point. Designs are smaller than traditional Village buildings and include Display Anywhere Lighting.

The Terry Redlin subseries includes three six lighted building and two wreaths in a fall setting that were gleaned from the works of renowned outdoors artist Terry Redlin (1937-2016).
