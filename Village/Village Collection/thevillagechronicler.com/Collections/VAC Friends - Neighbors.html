Friends & Neighbors
Revised 11/7/2025

In 2024, collector prayers were answered. There had long been a desire for village residents who were engaged in various activities to add to collector displays. These various sets were incorporated into many of the Gold Standard Villages as sets of three figurines. While the activity of some is generic in nature, there are those that were designed with specific buildings in mind. In as much as they are a continuing subset of the villages, they are referenced here along with a description of each figure in the set.
