The Original Snow Village Signs
Revised 2/11/2020

Throughout the history of The Original Snow Village, as with other collections, Department 56 has created village specific signs that can be incorporated into collector displays. The Original Snow Village signs are all ceramic and feature free-standing signs as well as one with children and another as part of the village gate offerings.
