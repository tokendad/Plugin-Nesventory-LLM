Cold Cast Porcelain (CCP)- Dickens' Village
Revised 6/17/2020

In 1986, Department 56 released two new collections made of resin- Victorian Miniatures and Williamsburg Miniatures. These buildings were not based on existing village buildings or and large scale porcelain collection.

The Dickens' Village Miniatures were released in 1987 and consists of twenty-five buildings based off of numerous designs of the time. It is the largest of the CCP collections and includes six sets as well as individual items. Each building was wrapped in bubble wrap and packaged in individual, heavy corrugated, top-load cardboard boxes with black and white graphics of the building on the front.
