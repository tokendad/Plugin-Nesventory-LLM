Churches
Revised 1/15/2021

Traditionally, churches are the center of all communities. Bavarian churches are no exception. They are often beautiful examples of unique architecture, colors, and frescos. The Alpine Village has had several churches inspired by actual ones that still stand to this day. The original ones were designed by Department 56 Master Architect Neilan Lund. Latter ones were designed by Barb Lund severaland Tom Bates.

For the purpose of this list and due to the high collectability of churches by collectors, all religious buildings and coordinating religious themed accessories are found here.
