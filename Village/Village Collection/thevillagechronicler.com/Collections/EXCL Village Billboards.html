Village Billboards
Revised 6/17/2020

There have been nine tailored/custom billboards sold by Platinum Key Dealers. Each design is unique to the specific retailer featuring original artwork tied to the store or the state in which it operates. One tailored version was also offered for sale by the Chicago Gathering in 2015 and Department 56 also tailored one with the Department 56 logo to be used as a retail event signing item. Production of all billboards is extremely limited and low in number.
