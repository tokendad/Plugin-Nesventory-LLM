Santa Comes To Town Series
Revised 11/7/2025

Santa Comes To Town is an ongoing ceramic series that was introduced in 1995. Each design features a traditional Santa interacting, most often, with children in a variety of activities. Each is a Limited To Year of Production piece. The 2019 design is a 25th Anniversary Commemorative of the series and features silver accents.
