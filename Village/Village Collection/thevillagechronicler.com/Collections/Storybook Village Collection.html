Storybook Village Collection
Revised 6/17/2020

The Storybook Village Collection is comprised of lit building sets with Coordinating Accessories included. All are light-hearted Department 56 artist interpretations of storybook characters, nursery rhymes, biblical characters, and renowned licensed characters. All designs were created in resin which later evolved into a few done in ceramic. All were part of the giftware line. Department 56 numbering system accounts for the inconsistency of numbering versus introduction date.
