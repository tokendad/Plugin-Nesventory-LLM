Haunted Rails
Revised 1/25/2024

The Haunted Rails subseries was, initially, introduced as a small, non-moving train set for the 10th anniversary of Snow Village Halloween. By 2012, it had grown into an ongoing series of train cars introduced at midyear. The collection includes the station, outbuildings, train cars, and numerous Coordinating Accessories for the train cars. It should be noted that the animated Haunted Coal Car set was introduced seven years prior to the subseries; it is a natural fit for it and is considered by most collectors to be part of the collection.

The entire collection was ultimately retired in 2019. In 2023, for the 25th anniversary of the collection, Department 56 released a Special Edition train car and coordinating accessory to commemorate the occasion.
