12 Days Of Dickens' Village Accessories
Revised 8/21/2019

The 12 Days of Dickens' Village Accessory series is a porcelain collection inspired by the Christmas Carol “The Twelve Days of Christmas.” Each piece features gold accents in some form or fashion in its design. Through the years there has been conversation regarding the artist's interpretation of the designs for the tenth and eleventh in the series due to the many interpretations of the song, itself. The collection is often used by collectors in conjunction with the Twelfth Night subseries which is also inspired, to some degree, by the iconic song.





Artist- Kristi Jensen Pierro
