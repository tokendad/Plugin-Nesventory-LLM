The Enesco Gallery and Gift Shop Featuring Department 56
Revised 10/2/2025

In coordination with the opening of The Enesco Gallery and Gift Shop Featuring Department 56, a new and updated version of Graceland, featuring LED lighting, along with a coordinating accessory of The Lisa Marie, were introduced as part of The Graceland Collection. For the Grand Opening of the facility both pieces were signed by Department 56 artists Scott Enter and Tom Bates. At a separate event in 2024, Master Sculptor Krist Namtvedt appeared as part of Elvis' Birthday Weekend and signed the airplane.

In addition to the Graceland property releases in 2023, a tailored version of two Mickey's Merry Christmas Village items were made for exclusive sale at the Enesco Gallery. All pieces are marked with the special Graceland Collection bottom stamps.

In 2025, Possible Dreams Artist Ann Dezendorf designed a tailored version of Christmas Grooves (#610085) featuring Elvis’ most popular Christmas album. As with other items in the Graceland Collection, it is only available at the Enesco Gift Gallery in Memphis, TN.
