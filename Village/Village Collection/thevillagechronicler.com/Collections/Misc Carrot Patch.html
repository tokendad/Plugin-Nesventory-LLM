Carrot Patch
Revised 6/17/2020

Carrot Patch is an ongoing, Easter themed giftware collection featuring bunnies at work and play. Each porcelain bunny figurine is accented with muted pastel colors. Coordinating garden accessories, music boxes, and waterglobes were also produced. The collection began in 1988 and continued until 1995.
