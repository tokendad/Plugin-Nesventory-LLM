The Heritage Village
Revised 10/31/2024

Both new and old collectors are often confused by the Heritage Village classification. It is a comprehensive term that is used to encompass all of the porcelain village collections in an effort to differentiate them from the ceramic items in The Original Snow Village. This category of products includes those items that are easily interchangeable in or used as standalones in any of the porcelain collections. Items below are divided by category and listed in year of release order. Inconsistency in numbers is attributed to the Department 56 numbering structure.

*In 1997, tailored version of Our Own Village Park Bench offered for sale by some Gold Key Dealers. They may be found under the Commissions/Exclusive listings.
