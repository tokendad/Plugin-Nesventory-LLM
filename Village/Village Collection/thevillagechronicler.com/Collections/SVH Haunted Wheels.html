Haunted Wheels
Revised 11/4/2023

Expanding on the Food Truck “craze”, the Haunted Wheels subseries was added to Snow Village Halloween in 2019. Each lit truck features a truly delectable specialty along with a Coordinating Accessories that is sure to make your stomach churn. The food trucks are considered lit accessories and not buildings. The entire subseries was retired in 2023.
