Pine Isles 
Revised 6/17/2020

Pine Isles is a collection of heavy, snow-laden, porcelain buildings with Coordinating Accessories reminiscent of a woodland camp setting. The continuing collection was produced for and sold exclusively by Kohl's over three Christmas seasons.
