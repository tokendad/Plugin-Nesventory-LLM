Baby/New Baby
Revised 10/5/2021

Occasionally, throughout its history, Department has introduced specific holiday inspired designs that go beyond Christmas. The majority of the items below were made available for sale during specific Spring Events promoted by Department 56 for its dealers. Many of these items are packaged gift sets that include buildings and Coordinating Accessories in a spring setting and no snow. For collectors who like to display themed items for specific holidays, seasons, and special occasions those pieces are noted here.

This listing references the building along with its Coordinating Accessories (if so designed). Some items may not have a COORDINATING BUILDING or accessory. All of them reference the specific collection for which they were designed and where they may be found. Accessories contained in specific gift sets are not noted separately.
