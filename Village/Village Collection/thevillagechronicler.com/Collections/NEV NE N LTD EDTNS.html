NEW ENGLAND VILLAGE- Numbered Limited Editions
Revised 8/21/2019

New England Village, like other village collections, has had several numbered limited editions introduced over its run. Each are meticulously designed and encompass a variety of building designs synonymous with the period. The two coordinating accessories released in 2018 and 2020 are referenced as general release items.
