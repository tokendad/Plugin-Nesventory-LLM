Legendary Ballparks
Revised 6/17/2020

The Legendary Ballparks subseries includes replica façades of America's most popular and recognizable ballparks. The back of each lighted building lists historical information for each. This collection is not to be confused with the Accents Baseball Ballparks collection which are much smaller in size.

This subseries also includes Souvenir Shops, Taverns, and Refreshment Stands with the logos of each respective team. Each structure is licensed by Major League Baseball Properties, Inc. Coordinating Accessories were designed that are universal in nature with the exception of two.
