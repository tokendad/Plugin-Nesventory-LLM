CHRISTMAS IN THE CITY- Limited to Year of Production
Revised 8/20/2020

Christmas In The City, like other village collections, has had several buildings and holiday gift sets introduced that were limited to a specific year of production. These designs cover a wide array of significant events, partnerships, anniversaries, as well as those offered during annual holiday retailer events. All accessories contained in the special event sets are referenced with their respective set. Accessories which coordinate and compliment designs are listed separately.
