Norman Rockwell
Revised 9/21/2020

The iconic artwork of American painter and illustrator Norm Rockwell served as inspiration for this subseries in The Original Snow Village. He is equally known for his portrayal of everyday life on the cover of The Saturday Evening Post. His paintings and cover work were incorporated into these offerings.
