Jim Shore Heartwood Creek
Revised 1/15/2025

The Jim Shore Heartwood Creek collection was introduced in 2011 and is designed by Enesco Folk Artist Jim Shore, personally. It includes several lit buildings and Coordinating Accessories which are all made of resin and feature the quilted and rosemaling accents he is known for. The series came to an abrupt end after two new buildings and three accessories were announced with the 2012 introductions. None of the five were ever produced. In 2019, Jim Shore furthered his relationship with Department 56 by designing a new subseries for New England Village.
