Light-Up Ornaments- New England Village
Revised 6/17/2020

In 1987, Department 56 released two separate sets of resin, clip-on, light-up ornaments to Dicken's Village and New England Village. The New England Village set was based on the success of all of the buildings released at that time. There were two groupings of individual ornaments with unique item numbers. All were placed on a red cardboard base encased in plastic. Each light-up has a metal clip to support it to a tree branch and a hole for a Christmas light to be placed in.
