Snow Village Halloween
Revised 10/29/2022

The Original Snow Village Artist Scott Enter had long-wanted to step into the fall and Halloween season with a village collection but was unsure of what approach to take. One day, caution was thrown to the wind when he reworked a ceramic house with a Halloween theme.

The test of success came with the simultaneous release of the black roofed and green roofed Haunted Mansion in featuring interior movement and sound. The black roof was introduced in the giftware line with special packaging and the green roof version into the core village line. Both pieces are considered by collectors to be the cornerstone of the village along with the first accessory set Trick Or Treat Kids (s/3).

Numerous subseries' may be found amongst the witches, ghosts, ghouls, and goblins of Snow Village Halloween. They include the over-decorated homes of Trick-Or-Lane, Day Of The Dead, Wiches Hollow, the dated Rest In Peace Crypts, Ghost Town, the Haunted Rails Train, Haunted Wheels Food Trucks, and many more.

For the 10th Anniversary of Snow Village Halloween, numerous items were produced. A small, ongoing series of four ceramic accessories were also introduced in honor of the 10th anniversary as well as a collection sign.
