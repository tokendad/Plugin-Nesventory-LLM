DICKENS' VILLAGE- Original Seven
Revised 11/19/2022

The Dickens' Village series was released in 1984 and designated as part of the Heritage Village Collection due to its porcelain makeup. The original set of seven buildings were designed by Neilan Lund and were also released under one pre-pack SKU number (65153). The inspiration for the collection was Victorian London during the times of Charles Dickens.

The Coordinating Accessories Carolers (s/3) was the only accessories introduced the same year. There are three variations of the set. The first issue contains a man leaning against a bare porcelain post facing the opposite direction of the latter two issues.
