Crayola
Revised 1/17/2025

At some point in life, every child wishes for and gets Crayola Crayons in their stocking. In 2000, a licensing agreement was made with Binney & Smith for inclusion in North Pole. For 2021, a new agreement was reached with Hallmark, the current owner of the brand, for new designs.
