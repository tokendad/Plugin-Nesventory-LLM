Buck's County
Revised 6/10/2019

Buck's County is a small subseries inspired by the rural farming area of Buck's County, PA. Offerings have their own distinct logo on the outer wrapper. Two buildings come as sets with Coordinating Accessories enclosed as noted. Several Coordinating Accessories are made of resin rather than the standard ceramic.
