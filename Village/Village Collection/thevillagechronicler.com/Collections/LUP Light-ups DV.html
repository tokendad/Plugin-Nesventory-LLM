Light-Up Ornaments- Dickens' Village
Revised 6/17/2020

In 1987, Department 56 released two separate sets of resin, clip-on, lightupup ornaments to Dicken's Village and New England Village. The Dickens' Village set was based on the success of all of the buildings released at that time. There were three groupings of individual ornaments. All but one was placed on a green cardboard base encased in plastic. The A Christmas Carol (s/3) was on a red base with individual partitions between the three ornaments to protect them from breakage.
