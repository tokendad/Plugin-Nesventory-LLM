Portobello Road Thatched Cottages
Revised 8/21/2019

The Portobello Road Thatched Cottages are a small subset of Dickens' Village. Referenced as a set of three (#58246), each building has a distinct item number and was sold, individually, as well. The collection is named for a street in west London that is well-known for its Saturday street markets featuring antiquities.
