2025 Department 56 Appearances

 

All Thru the House is Proud to announce that Department 56s own Ms. Lit Town, Melinda Seegers will be the guest of honor at our 2nd Annual Village Event on Saturday, August 11th (more details coming).

Melinda always brings a passion with her for the collectors and for the Villages. She is a veritable treasure trove of D56 information. She truly is the foremost authority on Village.

The exciting day will include an outstanding seminar by Melinda and she will be hosting several “Village Games” throughout the day. Lunch will be provided by our local collectors club The Arkansas Villagers. There will be great promotions and a chance to check out some of the new Village pieces.

Please call the store at 501-907-8999 (or send us a message) to RSVP. Not required.
