The Collections
 Revised 11/15/2025
The Collections listing consists of the individual subseries’ to each respective Gold Standard Village collection. The listing for each subseries includes the individual lighted buildings and coordinating accessories by item number, as well as the year of introduction and retirement for each. Specifically designed accessories are referenced to the building(s) for which they are designed.

The Collections listing also references items that were produced as a specific category, theme or specialty item which were designed to complement the Gold Standard Villages. These items are considered separate from the respective village collections that are found in The Stacks. The Holidays, Seasons, and Occasions listing groups those items designed for a specific holiday, season, and occasion across all Gold Standard Village lines.

We currently have 11,885 items in our database.


Items that have a (Dash and a letter) following the item number are items that are part of a set.

 If you are not sure how to find what you are looking for click on the link below and then click on the "All Products" link, and follow the search instructions on that page.
