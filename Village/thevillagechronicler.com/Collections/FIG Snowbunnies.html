Snowbunnies
Revised 5/19/2024

Snowbunnies, originally contained in white storybook boxes with pink lettering, hopped into the collector scene in 1995 (designs were copyrighted in 1994). Inside each cover is a poem entitled “Springtime Stories of the Snowbunnies”. Snowbunnies are the springtime cousins of the Snowbabies featuring blues eyes, bunny ears, cotton tails, and “nubbies”. They are frequently captured in childlike wonder with all things related to an Easter setting and spring accented with pastel colors. Numerous ancillary items were also introduced as part of the line. These playful characters are another one of Kristi Jensen-Pierro's whimsical figurine collections. Inconsistent numbering is attributed to Department 56 releases. The entire collection was discontinued in 2012.
