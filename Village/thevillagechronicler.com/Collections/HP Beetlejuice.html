Beetlejuice
Revised 10/7/2024

Beetlejuice was released in 1998 and is a fantasy horror comedy film directed by Tim Burton. This cult classic, distributed by Warner Bros, tells the story of a deceased couple, who as ghosts, reach out to Beetlejuice, a bio-exorcist, to rid their home of its new residents.

In 2024, Department 56 introduced a small village collection with two principal pieces offered for Early Release sale only through its Platinum Key Dealers.
