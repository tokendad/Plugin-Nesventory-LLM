Merry Makers
Revised 5/15/2025

The Merry Makers collection was designed by Department 56 Snowbabies Artist Kristi Jensen Pierro. The whimsical porcelain monks are approximately four inches in height and are captured enjoying all aspects of their monastery life from making sweets and cider, to outdoor fun and playing musical instruments. The collection also features several of the monks as bell ornaments and in waterglobes. Numerous porcelain accessories were also designed for the collection.

The Merrily-We-Roll-Along Carolers & Gabriel the Goat along with the porcelain church and white papier-mâché Chapel are substantial pieces in the collection and are highly sought after. The Merry Makers Retail Display Stand (#93910) was not offered for retail sales but can, occasionally, be found on the secondary market.
