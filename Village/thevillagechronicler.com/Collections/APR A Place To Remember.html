A Place To Remember
Revised 6/17/2020

A Place To Remember is one of a only a few introductions to ever be announced and not produced. The collection of LED lit facades is based on buildings synonymous with those found in Tuscany, Carmel, and Paris. Initially, introduced in 2010, items were re-introduced in 2011 with different item numbers and names as pictured in the company Product Brochures. They are listed as a matter of resource in the event that the company samples are found on the secondary market.
