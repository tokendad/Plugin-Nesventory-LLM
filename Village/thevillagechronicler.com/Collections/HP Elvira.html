Elvira
Revised 1/17/2025

In 2021 a licensing agreement with Queen “B” Productions allowed for horror icon Elvira- Mistress of the Dark to be added to the Department 56 Village family. The lighted building, with flying bats, along with Elvira walking her trusty companion, are ideal crossovers for the Snow Village Halloween series or as standalones.
