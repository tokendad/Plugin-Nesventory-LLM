Tin Lithograph Ornaments
Revised 6/17/2020

In conjunction with the release of Dickens' Village, Department 56 released six tin ornaments based off of Neilan Lund's original art. There has been long speculation that the Candle Shop was added to the Village as an afterthought.

Since all of the items have the same product number a suffix was added to make them unique.
