Restaurants
Revised 1/15/2025

New York is renowned for its countless, wide array of restaurants featuring every type of cuisine imaginable. From cultural favorites to fine dining, Christmas In The City is populated with them all. Each building features unique architecture synonymous with its offering. This is the longest on-going subseries in Christmas In The City. While taverns, nightclubs, and pubs do serve food, they are not included here.
