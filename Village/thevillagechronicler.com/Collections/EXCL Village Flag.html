Village Flags
Revised 6/17/2020

There have been eight tailored/custom versions of Stars & Stripes, #4038808 (GVA). Each flag is unique to the state flag in which a specific Platinum Key Dealer operates. Production of all state flags is extremely limited and low in number. The Department 56 Flag is a replica of the one that flew over One Village Place in Eden Prairie, MN and is the only one available as a General Release.
