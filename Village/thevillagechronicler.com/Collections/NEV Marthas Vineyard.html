Martha's Vineyard
Revised 8/21/2020

Located in the Atlantic Ocean south of Cape Cod, MA, Martha's Vineyard is a harbor town dotted with lighthouses, sandy beaches and farmland. In January 2020, Martha's Vineyard was introduced as a subseries the New England Village and subsequently retired with the entire collection later in the year. The two lit buildings are named for Edgartown, the first colonial settlement of the island. The lighthouse was inspired by one of the five that dot the coastline.
