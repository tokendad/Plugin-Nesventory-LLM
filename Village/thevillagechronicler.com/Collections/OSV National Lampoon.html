National Lampoon's Christmas Vacation
Revised 6/27/2025

The 1989 holiday film featuring Chevy Chase as Clark Griswold has become a Christmas classic. The movie is centered around Clark's soul desire to have a good old family Christmas despite the mishaps of family get-togethers. This licensed subseries to The Original Snow Village has become extremely popular amongst collectors for incorporation into their village and as a stand-alone.
