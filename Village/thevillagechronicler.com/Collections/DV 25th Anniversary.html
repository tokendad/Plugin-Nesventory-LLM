25th Anniversary- Dickens' Village
Revised 4/14/2020

For the 25th Anniversary of Dickens' Village, a first occurred. The Original Seven buildings designed by Master Village Architect Neilan Lund were updated and reimagined by his daughter Village Artist Barb Lund as a tribute to him. Several other pieces, tied to Charles Dickens were also introduced.
