All Through The House
Revised 5/15/2025

The All Thru the House giftware collection was designed by Department 56 Artist Kristi Jensen-Pierro. The collection gives insight as to the activity inside of a busy home on the night before Christmas. While there are numerous individual figures, there are also several wall scenes of the interior of the home. These walls have 3D exterior scenes as well. When they are placed together, they form the exterior of a beautiful red plank-sided home.





Artist Kristi Jensen Pierro
