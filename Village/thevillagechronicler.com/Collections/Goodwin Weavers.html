Goodwin Weaver Throws & Pillows
Revised 6/17/2020

Department 56 entered into a license agreement in 1994 with Crown Crafts, Inc. to produce 100% cotton throws, tree skirts, and pillows through its Goodwin Weavers Division. Each item features artwork of buildings and/or Snowbabies.
