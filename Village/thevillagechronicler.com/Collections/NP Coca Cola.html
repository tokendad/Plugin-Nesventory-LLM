Coca-Cola
Revised 5/19/2020

One of the longest and oldest licensing agreements that Department 56 has had has been with Coca-Cola. The first introductions to North Pole featured a bubbling bottle in 2002, along with an animated sliding hill and two other Coordinating Accessories. After a long break at the North Pole, new items were introduced featuring the elves manufacturing and enjoying their favorite soda, along with the iconic Coca-Cola bears.
