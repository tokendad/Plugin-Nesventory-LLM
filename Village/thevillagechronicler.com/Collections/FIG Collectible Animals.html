Easter/Annual Collectible Animals
Revised 8/22/2023

In 1991, Department 56 introduced a series of annual Easter Animals that were made of bisque porcelain, some with “nubbies”, like those found on Snowbabies and Snowbunnies. Each animal was produced as a Limited To Year of Production item in the giftware line. After the retirement of the Snowbunnies line, the collection fell under the Woodland Spring classification and then later under the Snowbabies umbrella. Kristi Jensen-Pierro is the artist of the Annual Collectible Animal Ornaments.

Items that share the same item number will be suffixed by a Dash & a Letter to make them unique.
