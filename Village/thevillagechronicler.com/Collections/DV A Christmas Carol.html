A Christmas Carol / A Christmas Carol Revisited
Revised 11/4/2023

A Christmas Carol is the single-most, renowned work of author Charles Dickens and plays a pivotal role in Dickens' Village. Based in Victorian England during the Christmas season, it also serves as the inspiration for the collection.

Following the retirement of the initial three buildings in 1995, the subseries was re-introduced in 1996 as A Christmas Carol Revisited with richer colors and enhanced designs. By 2000, the initial reference to the subseries was resumed.

For reference, all pieces in the subseries are listed here with the A Christmas Carol Revisited pieces being specifically noted. Prior to the retirement of the entire subseries in 2023, A Christmas Carol was the longest ongoing subseries in Dickens' Village and all other Village collections, as well.
