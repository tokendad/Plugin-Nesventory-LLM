Spring/Summer- No snow
Revised 5/10/2021

While the main focus of Dickens' Village has been on the Victorian Era during winter, there have been several offerings made available in the collection that are not designed with elements of snow. This listing is focused on spring, summer, and the seashore. All Hallow's Eve items are a specific subseries devoted to a specific holiday and are not included here.
