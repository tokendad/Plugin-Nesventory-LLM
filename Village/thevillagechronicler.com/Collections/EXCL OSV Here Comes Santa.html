Here Comes Santa
Revised 6/17/2020

For the 20th Anniversary of Department 56 and the Original Snow Village, 24 Gold Key Dealers offered a ceramic accessory in 1996 featuring Santa Claus leading a parade of three children. As part of the design carries a banner in his right hand personalized for each dealer. Former parent company, Bachman's piece features a red banner with “Joy To The World” on it and the little girl wears a purple coat.
