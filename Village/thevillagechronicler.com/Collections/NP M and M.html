M & M's
Revised 11/7/2025

No holiday would be complete without M & M Candies! It is only natural that those “melts in your mouth, not in your hands!” delicacies would be manufactured at the North Pole. The first licensed building and accessories were introduced with the 2004 Mid-Year pieces. In 2005, a two buildings with coordinating accessories were introduced into the giftware line that are easily incorporated into North Pole. They may be found in The Stacks under Miscellaneous Collector Items.

Twenty years after the initial introduction, a new cottage and accessories were introduced, making the candy coated treats a North Pole subseries.
