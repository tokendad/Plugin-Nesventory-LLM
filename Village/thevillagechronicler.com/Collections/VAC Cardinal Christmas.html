Cardinal Christmas
Revised 1/15/2025

Cardinal Christmas accessories capture “red birds” in snow-scene accessories. It is said that, cardinals are visits from deceased relatives. All of the accessories are easily incorporated into other Village scenes as well. They are, also, ideal for The Original Snow Village Christmas Court, Lot 60. It should be noted that previously introduced accessories with a cardinal theme have been grandfathered into this listing in 2022.
