Tim Burton's The Nightmare Before Christmas Village
Revised 5/23/2025

The spooky Halloween side of Tim Burton's “The Nightmare Before Christmas” was introduced as a new village collection in 2017. The initial offering was introduced as sets featuring a lit resin building and Coordinating Accessories. A special 25th Anniversary piece with silver accents, Jack On Spiral Hill, was introduced in 2018. In 2020, Jack's adventure into Christmas Town emerged in several accessories.
