Platinum Key Dealer
Revised 6/27/2025

Platinum Key Dealer status began in 2014. This designation was for those very elite Department 56 retailers who were committed to the many different village lines through purchasing, display and sales. This status allows for early ordering/shipping, tailored items for exclusive sale, and special event pieces. This designation is available to American and Canadian retailers.

As has been true with previous dealer classifications; Platinum Key Dealers have had exclusively produced items that were sold only through their doors. Each item bears the special Platinum Key Dealer logo on the outer sleeve and the item itself. In some instances, the building has a coordinating accessory that may not have been exclusive to the Platinum Key Dealers. All coordinating accessories, regardless of classification, are listed here.
