Seasons Bay
Revised 6/17/2020

Seasons Bay was the first and only Village designed for year-round display. It was released in 1998 with Gold Key Dealers only receiving the First Edition pieces featuring gold accents. The overall design is that of the seasonal resorts that were famous family destinations at the beginning of the 20th century. Accessories are designed in pewter and are smaller in scale so as to capture more intricate details and in response to collectors' requests for accessories more in scale to the buildings. Accessories are grouped and designed by the four seasons, making them interchangeable for that specific time of year.
