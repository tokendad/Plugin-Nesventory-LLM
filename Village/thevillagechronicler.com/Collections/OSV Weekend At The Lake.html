Weekend At The Lake
Revised 6/10/2019

While the main focus of The Original Snow Village has always been Main Street, USA in the depth of winter, there have been several offerings made available under the collection umbrella that do not contain elements of winter. Weekend At The Lake is a very small subseries' inspired by weekend excursions to the lake. This subseries is an ideal companion to the Summertime subseries.
