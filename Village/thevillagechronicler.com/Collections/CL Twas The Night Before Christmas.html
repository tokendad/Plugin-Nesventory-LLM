‘Twas The Night Before Christmas
Revised 6/17/2020

The 1823 poem by Clement S. Moore, A Visit From St. Nicholas, served as the inspiration for this resin collection. All items were packaged and sold individually by Department 56 retailers with the exception the Holiday Special Value Gift Set (#03052) which was a yellow variation that was exclusive to National Account department stores.
