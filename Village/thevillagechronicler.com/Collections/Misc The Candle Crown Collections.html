The Candle Crown Collections
Revised 1/31/2025

The Candle Crown Collections are a series of finely sculpted candle extinguishers. Each ceramic design is packaged in a signature burgundy box with gold lettering with a satin lined interior. The collection was introduced during mid-year 2001 and was retired in 2004.
