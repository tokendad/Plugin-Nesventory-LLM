Summertime
Revised 6/10/2019

While the main focus of The Original Snow Village has always been Main Street, USA in the depth of winter, there have been several offerings made available under the collection umbrella that are not designed with elements of snow. Summertime is a small subseries' that actually began with accessories. Building and accessory designs feature and activities synonymous with the summer months. This subseries is an ideal companion to the Weekend At The Lake subseries.
