North Pole Series
Revised 10/29/2022

The North Pole Series was originally released in 1990 with three pieces and Coordinating Accessories that truly personified collector's expectations of what the first pieces should be included. With glittery snow-laden roofs and bundles of firewood on the stoops, the entire collection has grown to capture all of the colors and exciting activity that one would expect to find at the North Pole.

North Pole is home to numerous subseries' including the Christmas Around the World accessory collection, Crayola, Elf Land, Gingerbread Lane, Merry Lane Cottages, and Santa's Sweet Shops to name a few.

Since the introduction of the previous villages, collectors had long desired for a series devoted to the North Pole. In 1990, their requests were answered with the introduction of three porcelain buildings and Coordinating Accessories that would become a cherished favorite of collectors new and old. The Reindeer Barn and Elf Bunkhouse were sold as a set of two and individually.
