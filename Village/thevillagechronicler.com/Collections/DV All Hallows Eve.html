All Hallows' Eve
Revised 3/14/2021

All Hallows' Eve was Department 56's way of injecting a true, ongoing, fall-themed subseries into the Dickens' Village collection. It was designed to appeal to collectors for display during the Halloween season. All Hallows' Eve, also known as All Saint's Day, falls on October 31st and is a widely celebrated holiday that paved the way for what is now known as Halloween.
