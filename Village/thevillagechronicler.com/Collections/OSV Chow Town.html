Chow Town
Revised 8/21/2019

Chow Town is an ongoing subseries in The Original Snow Village. Each restaurant features design elements unique to its theme. There have been two instances in which a lit building and its Coordinating Accessories were repainted by Department 56 and auctioned at collector gatherings to raise money for charity. Those are one-of-a-kinds and specifically designated below.
