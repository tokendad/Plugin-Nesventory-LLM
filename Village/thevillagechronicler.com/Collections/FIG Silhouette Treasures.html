Silhouette Treasures, aka Winter Silhouettes & Lamplighter
Revised 5/12/2023

In 1988 this white, porcelain bisque collection was introduced as the Lamplighter collection and consisted of ten pieces. )They are noted by an asterisk “*” below.) The designs draw inspiration from 19th century Victorian times and has evolved under the names of Winter Silhouettes and Silhouette Treasures. While the primary basis of the collections are white, elements of red, gold and silver accentuate some pieces. The collection has expanded from figures to include music boxes, ornaments, water globes, candle holders, nightlights, and lighted scenes.
