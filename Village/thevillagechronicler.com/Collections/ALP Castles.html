Castles
Revised 9/16/2019

Thousands of castles are perched on the Bavarian mountaintops and dot the landscape. They are beautiful examples of architecture that runs prominent through the region. The Alpine Village has had several introductions inspired by them. The castles make great focal points to displays and are highly sought after.
