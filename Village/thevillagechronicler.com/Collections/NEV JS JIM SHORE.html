JIM SHORE
Revised 6/21/2019

In 2019, legendary folk artist Jim Shore, also under license with Enesco, designed a small subset of buildings for the New England Village. The four buildings in the subseries are true to his legacy and incorporate elements of his renowned Heartwood Creek and quilt-style designs.
