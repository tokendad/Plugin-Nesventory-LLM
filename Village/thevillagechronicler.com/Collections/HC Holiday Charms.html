Holiday Charms
Revised 9/24/2024

Holiday Charms is a village collection made of porcelain that was sold in packaged sets and individually. The small collection of packaged sets was sold exclusively by Linens-N-Things and contained a lighted building with a specifically designed silver charm, tree(s) and Coordinating Accessories. The collection was individually packaged and sold by Menard's. All were offered for sale during the 2003 Christmas season.
