Time To Celebrate- Merryville
Revised 6/17/2020

As part of its offerings was a small village series known as Merryville. Though it was a giftware collection, it is listed here because of its collectability as a small, lit village series. The collection includes sets with a building, accessories and trees as well as individual ancillary accessories. Several designs are easily incorporated into other village displays.
