Witch Hollow
Revised 11/7/2025

Witch Hollow is where the witch sisters Hilda, Agnes, Tillie, Lucy, and Hazel can be found supplying the needs of all of the witches in their coven. In buildings inspired by their hats and shoes, no cauldron will be empty after visiting Witch Hollow.
