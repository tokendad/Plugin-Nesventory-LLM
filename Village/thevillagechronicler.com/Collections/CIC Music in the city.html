Music In The City
Revised 4/21/2021

Introduced as a play off of the collection name, Music In The City is a subseries comprised of the many clubs found throughout the heart of a major metropolitan area. Each reflects a certain genre not only in the type of music but in the architecture as well. This short-lived subseries also has Coordinating Accessories for each building.
