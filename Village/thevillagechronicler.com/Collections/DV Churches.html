Dickens' Village Churches
Revised 11/4/2022

Through the history of Dickens' Village a church is one of the first purchases a collector makes or receives as a gift. Numerous churches, representing unique architecture and faiths, have been designed for the series. Due to the limited number of religious figures available, it is not uncommon for collectors to incorporate those from other collections.
