Chelsea Market Carts
Revised 1/15/2021

The Chelsea Market Carts subseries is inspired by the bustling market located in west London near the River Thames. Each porcelain accessory features a vendor cart and vendor. First introduced in 1993, this collection is perfectly displayed in mass market scenes together, with other vendors, or as individual peddlers throughout Dickens' Village displays. In 1993, a coordinating group of individual shoppers were also introduced.
