Monopoly Brand City Lights Collection
Revised 6/17/2020

The Monopoly Brands City Lights Collection is comprised of lighted, resin facades. It was introduced in 1999 as a complete set of eight or as individual items, with each building having a unique, two-sided disc and game token. Buildings placed side-by-side spell MONOPOLY and increase in size as your travel from left to right down the boardwalk. The collection was the first to offer a Redemption Piece with purchase.
