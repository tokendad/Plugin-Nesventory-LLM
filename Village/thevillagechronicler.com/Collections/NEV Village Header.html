New England Village 
Revised 10/29/2022

The New England Village series was released in 1986 and designated as part of the Heritage Village Collection due to its porcelain makeup. The original set of seven buildings were designed by Neilan Lund, with a smaller footprint, and were released under one SKU number.

The New England Village series is true to its name incorporating fall, harvest, coastal, and Amish elements found in the New England area of the United States.

Popular subseries' found in the New England Village are Sleepy Hollow, Coastal Lights, Salem Willows, and Founder's Square.
