Numbered Limited Editions
Revised 11/7/2025

Dickens' Village, like other village collections, has had numbered limited editions introduced over its long run. In 1985, it introduced the first hand numbered limited edition to any village series. Each building is meticulously crafted and encompasses a variety of building designs synonymous with Victorian London.

To date, there has been only one numbered limited edition accessory for Dickens' Village. There have been several Coordinating Accessories that were not numbered; yet, designed to coordinate with a specific building that was. Those accessories are designated last in this listing and are designated by italicized, bold small letters.
