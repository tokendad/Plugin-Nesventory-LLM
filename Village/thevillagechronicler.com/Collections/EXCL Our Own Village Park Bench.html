Our Own Village Park Bench - Gold Key Dealers
Revised 5/22/2025

In 1997, Gold Key Dealers were offered the opportunity to have an exclusive, customized version of Our Own Village Park Bench (HV, #02211). Dealers were given the choice of their store logo or a personalized message that could be adhesively applied to the bench back and sold exclusively through their doors. The Original Bench is listed here but is part of the Heritage Village classification. Due to the fact that Department 56 did not keep a running list of dealers who purchased the bench; new ones will be added as they are discovered.
