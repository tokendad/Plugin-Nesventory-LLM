Margaritaville 
Revised 6/10/2020

Jimmy Buffet's iconic song “Margaritaville” served as inspiration for this licensed, seaside collection. Buildings and accessories are made of resin. The Margaritaville Volcano Club features a real water-based smoking feature and Yes, I Am A Pirate will accommodate a small wireless speaker to play your favorite island tunes.
