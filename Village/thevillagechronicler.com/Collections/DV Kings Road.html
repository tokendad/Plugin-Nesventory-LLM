King's Road
Revised 8/21/2020

King's Road is a very prominent and major two mile street that runs through west London. It was actually considered the king's private road right up until the time of Queen Victoria. The original tow lit buildings were issued under one item number and sold as a set of two. Only two Coordinating Accessories were offered for these pieces.
