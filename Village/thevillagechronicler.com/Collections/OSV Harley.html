Harley-Davidson
Revised 6/23/2025

One of the earliest license agreements for The Original Snow Village was with the leader in the motorcycle industry, Harley-Davidson. While numerous buildings and accessories had been introduced over the years for other village series', the most expansive set of designs were for the Original Snow Village.
