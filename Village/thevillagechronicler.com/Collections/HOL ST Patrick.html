St. Patrick's Day / Irish
Revised 5/16/2021

Occasionally, throughout its history, Department has introduced specific holiday inspired designs that go beyond Christmas. There are a few items that are St. Patricks' Day or Irish themed. For collectors who like to display themed items for specific holidays, seasons, and special occasions those pieces are noted here.

This listing references the building along with its Coordinating Accessories (if so designed). Some items may not have a COORDINATING BUILDING or accessory. All of them reference the specific collection for which they were designed and where they may be found. Accessories contained in specific gift sets are not noted separately.
