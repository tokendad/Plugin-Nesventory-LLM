Peanuts Holiday Village
Revised 1/7/2025

The Peanuts Holiday Village was inspired by the iconic characters created by one of Minnesota's favorite sons, Charles M. Schulz. The 45th Anniversary of A Charlie Brown Christmas and the 60th anniversary of the long-running comic strip served as the perfect time for Department 56 to introduce this popular collection. Several pieces were offered as Early Releases to Sears.
