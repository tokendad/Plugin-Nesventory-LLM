Snow Village Halloween- Numbered Limited Editions
Revised 11/7/2025

Snow Village Halloween, like other village collections, has had numbered limited editions introduced over its run. Granted, due to its issue size there have been a fewer number. Each are meticulously designed and encompass a variety of building designs. The Coordinating Accessories were not numbered or limited and the accessory contained in the boxed set was not named by the company.
