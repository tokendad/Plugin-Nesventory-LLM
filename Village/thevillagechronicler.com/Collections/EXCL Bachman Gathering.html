Bachman's Collector Gathering Exclusives
Revised 6/17/2020

As the former parent company of Department 56, Bachman's, a Minneapolis Florist, has been a long-standing retail account for the company. Over the years, numerous items have been exclusively produced for them to sell in their locations as well as during their hosted collector gatherings in the 1990s.

In the late 1990s, Bachman's established and hosted several Department 56 collector gatherings. During these events, numerous buildings and accessories were made available for purchase during them. In 1999, Bachman's Homestead, inspired by the original Bachman home, became the first Hand Numbered Limited Edition to be offered for The Original Snow Village.
