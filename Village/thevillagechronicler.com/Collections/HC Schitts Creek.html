Schitt's Creek
Revised 1/4/2024

Schitt's Creek was a CBC Television show that ran from 2015 through 2020. The show tells the story of the once famous and now destitute Rose family. They are cast from their opulent lifestyle into a small country town that they once purchased as a joke. They, in turn, are forced to take up residence in the Rosebud Motel which becomes the spring board to their reclamation of their lifestyle. Over its six seasons and some 80 episodes, the show garnered numerous accolades and amassed an extensive following. With that, Department 56 introduced a resin village collection beginning with the Motel and the town's iconic billcoard.
