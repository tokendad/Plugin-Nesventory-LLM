Coca-Cola
Revised 5/20/2020

One of the longest and oldest licensing agreements that Department 56 has had has been with Coca-Cola. It all started with the release of the Coca-Cola Brand Bottling Plant in 1994. The license agreement has expanded to include introductions for Christmas In The City and the North Pole Series.
