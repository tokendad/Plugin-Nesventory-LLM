Rest In Peace Crypts (2013-Current)
Revised 11/4/2023

The popularity of the Snow Village Halloween collection paved the way for an ongoing series of cemetery mausoleum crypts. The subseries was formally introduced with the Rest in Peace, 2013.

The crypts were introduced in a variety of ways including standalones, gift sets, and with coordinating accessories. Each are dated numerically or with Roman numerals. They are referenced here as such and by their distinguishable features.
