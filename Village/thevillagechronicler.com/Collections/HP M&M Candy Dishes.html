M&M Lighted Candy Dishes
Revised 6/13/2020

The M&M Candy Dishes are licensed with M&M Mars and designed to hold M&M Candy. While these items are not considered Villages, they are a small giftware collection that collectors often use to accent their home and collection. Seven of these are licensed through other entities or Department 56 original designs and considered plain candy dishes.
