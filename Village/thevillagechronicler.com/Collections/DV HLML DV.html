Historical Landmark Series- Dickens' Village
Revised 2/12/2019

The Historical Landmark series began in 1997 with the issuance of two of the most well-renowned buildings in London. This new classification expanded to include other iconic buildings that grace the London skyline as well as others throughout the world. Offerings extend across Village lines and some have Coordinating Accessories that were offered separately.
