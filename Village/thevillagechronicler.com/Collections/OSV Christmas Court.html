Christmas Court
Revised 1/17/2025

The subseries Christmas Court is inspired by and invokes memories of the mobile home trailer parks that were prominent during the 1950s. Naturally, the first in the series was a reference to “56” with subsequent issues following the numerical sequence. One design was issued as a Platinum Key Dealer Exclusive in 2017.
