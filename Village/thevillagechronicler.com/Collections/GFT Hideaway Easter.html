Hide-A-Way Hollow- Spring
Revised 6/17/2020

Hide-A-Way Hollow is a small series of resin building facades that was part of the giftware line. As a Spring themed series, it featured pewter rabbit accessories that were made to coordinate with and fit inside of the buildings. The collection is packaged in green boxes with acrylic sheet fronts. The entire series was introduced in 1989 and retired in 1992.
