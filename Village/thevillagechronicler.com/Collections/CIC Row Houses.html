Row Houses & Brownstones
Revised 10/9/2023

Row houses, town houses, and brownstones are synonymous with New York neighborhoods. Each has unique features. For the purpose of Christmas In The City, all styles are listed here. While a few are part of gift sets with Coordinating Accessories, only a few have specifically designed accessories. With the abundance of accessories designed for Christmas In The City, any number of them could be incorporated into a festive, busy neighborhood scene.
