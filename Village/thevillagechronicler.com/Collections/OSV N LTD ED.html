The Original Snow Village- Numbered Limited Editions
Revised 10/11/2024

The Original Snow Village, like other village collections, has had numerous numbered limited editions introduced over its run. They encompass a variety of building designs with different motifs- some without snow. The first numbered limited edition for this village was released in 1998 as part of a promotion to raise money for Ronald McDonald charities. Only one numbered accessory has been issued to date.
