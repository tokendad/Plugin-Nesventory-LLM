NEW ENGLAND VILLAGE- Limited to Year of Production
Revised 8/21/2019

New England Village, like other village collections, has had several buildings and holiday gift sets introduced that were limited to a specific year of production. While two designs were introduced to mark special occasions in New England Village and Department 56 history, the majority were offered during annual holiday retailer events. There have been several Limited to Year of Production accessories commemorating Alpine Village and Department 56 history, and for posterity purposes, those contained in the gift sets are listed here as well.
