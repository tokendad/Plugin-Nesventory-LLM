Snow Village Halloween Signs
Revised 1/4/2022

Throughout the history of Snow Village Halloween, as with other collections, Department 56 has created village specific signs that can be incorporated into collector displays. The Snow Village Halloween signs are free-standing signs and one is part of the village gate offerings. Directional signs and street signs are not part of this listing.
