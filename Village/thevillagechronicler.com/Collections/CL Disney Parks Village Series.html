Disney Parks Village Series
Revised 6/17/2020

The Disney Parks Village Series was introduced in 1994. It was done so in partnership with the Disney Theme Parks in California and Florida. The designs are based on actual buildings found, and so named, in the parks at that time and include Coordinating Accessories.
