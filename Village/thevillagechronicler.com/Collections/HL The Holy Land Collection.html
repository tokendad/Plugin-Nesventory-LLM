The Holy Land Collection
Revised 6/26/2020

The Holy Land Collection is an umbrella classification that encompasses all of the individual lighted buildings and accessories surrounding the birth, ministry, and death of Jesus Christ as well as the specific individual buildings and accessories released in 2000 onward.
