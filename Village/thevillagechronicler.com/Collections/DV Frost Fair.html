Frost Fair
Revised 8/21/2019

The Frost Fair subseries of Dickens' Village captures the essence of the pop-up fairs that were held when the Thames River froze over in London. The first building and accessory introductions were repainted versions of the General Release ones that were only available from Platinum Key Dealers. The small subseries was introduced in 2014 and retired in 2016.
