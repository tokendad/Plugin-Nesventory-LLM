S-A-N-T-A
Revised 6/10/2019

For the 15th Anniversary of the North Pole series, a familiar reference was used for the buildings that were introduced. Each featured an elf proprietors' first name that spelled out “SANTA”. The buildings also are accentuated with a custom gold initial weathervane that when displayed together, spells out “SANTA” as well.

This subseries also features some unique features in that the first building was made available as an Early Release item that was only available through Club 56 Dealers. The second building, Alfies Toy School For Elves, was only offered online, directly from Department 56. The color palette for each was changed and were also introduced as General Release items for all dealers. Santa's Toy Company was released as a Hand Numbered Limited Edition with all others being released as Limited to Year of Production pieces. All Coordinating Accessories were released as General release items and do not have special features.
