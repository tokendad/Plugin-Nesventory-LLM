Disney's Mickey's Merry Christmas Village
Revised 9/12/2025

One of the longest ongoing license agreements with Department 56 is with Disney. The popularity of the Mickey, Minnie and all of their friends made for a perfect holiday village series between the two companies. While the buildings are made of porcelain, the Coordinating Accessories figures are made of resin to capture every detail of the characters. Sears had the early introduction of Mickey's Merry Christmas Village Series from the onset and has enjoyed many years of Early Releases as well as an Exclusive Release.
