Valentine's Day
Revised 3/9/2024

Occasionally, throughout its history, Department has introduced specific holiday inspired designs that go beyond Christmas. While the Celebrate Love series was introduced for The Original Snow Village in 2003, there have been designs that reflect Valentine's Day in other collections. For collectors who like to display themed items for specific holidays, seasons, and special occasions those pieces are noted here.

This listing references the building along with its coordinating accessory (if so designed). Some items may not have a coordinating buildings or accessory. All of them reference the specific collection for which they were designed and where they may be found. Accessories contained in specific gift sets are not noted separately.
