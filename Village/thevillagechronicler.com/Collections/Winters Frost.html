Winters Frost
Revised 6/23/2020

The Winters Frost collection was created by Snow Village Artist Scott Enter who was inspired by the glistening snow on the rooftops and trees that he saw on a drive one winter morning. Each building comes with its own adapter and there are no Coordinating Accessories.
