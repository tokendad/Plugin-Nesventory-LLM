Reindeer Stables
Revised 6/17/2020

As the North Pole expanded, it was only natural that additions would be made to include new stables for Santa's reindeer. Each lighted building features signs with the attribute that each reindeer brings to Santa's team. This ongoing subseries is easily incorporated into North Pole or used as a stand-alone.
