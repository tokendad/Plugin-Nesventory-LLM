A Victorian Christmas
Revised 8/21/2019

A Victorian Christmas is a subseries Dickens' Village inspired by a gift set that was introduced in 2004. Each piece is designed to capture the spirit of Victorian holiday traditions and includes individual buildings, gift sets as well as Coordinating Accessories that are free-standing and animated.
