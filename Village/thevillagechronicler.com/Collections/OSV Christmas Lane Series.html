Christmas Lane
Revised 10/7/2024

The Christmas Lane subseries is inspired by over-decorated homes during the holiday season. Every neighborhood has at least one and sometimes an entire neighborhood or community follows suit. Each lit building design features a specific over-decorated theme and has a Coordinating Accessories. This is one of the longest running subseries' in The Original Snow Village. Christmas Lane is inspired by and captures the feel of the over-decorated home in every town. Each home has Coordinating Accessories that accent the theme of each home. The Holiday House is not, officially, a member of Christmas Lane but is considered the cornerstone of the subseries.
