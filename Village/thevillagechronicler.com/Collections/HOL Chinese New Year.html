Chinese New Year / Chinatown
Revised 11/7/2025

Occasionally, throughout its history, Department has introduced specific holiday inspired designs that go beyond Christmas. There are items primarily designed for the Chinese New Year and others that references Chinatown and/or has Asian influence. For collectors who like to display themed items for specific holidays, seasons, and special occasions those pieces are noted here.

This listing references the building along with its Coordinating Accessories (if so designed). Some items may not have a COORDINATING BUILDING or accessory. All of them reference the specific collection for which they were designed and where they may be found. Accessories contained in specific gift sets are not noted separately.
