Trick Or Treat Lane
Revised 1/7/2025

The Trick Or Treat Lane subseries was inspired by the successful release of the Christmas Lane subseries in The Original Snow Village. Like its counterpart, Trick Or Treat Lane is comprised of over-decorated homes; just for the Halloween season. Each lit building design, sometimes in sets, features a specific over-decorated theme and has at least one Coordinating Accessories. There was no design offered in 2023.
