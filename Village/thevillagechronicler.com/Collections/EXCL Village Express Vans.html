Village Express Vans
Revised 7/21/2025

The Village Express Vans are a 2/3 replica of a 1929 British Morris Minor Van that founder Ed Bazinet had built for the Department 56 Showroom at One Village Place. The van made numerous appearances at trade shows, conventions and special events. The original version (#58563) was introduced as a Heritage Village General Accessory and is why it appears as green- for the classification.

Over the years, the Village Express Van has taken on many different looks recognizing holidays, anniversaries and special occasions. A complete boxed set of Holiday Express Vans (#06015) was given to Department 56 Representatives and Employees for Christmas in 2002. In 2018, the first ceramic version was included in the Tribute Gift Set B-Sweet Shop, #6002956 (OSV).
