Oliver Twist
Revised 7/14/2018

The subseries David Copperfield is based on the Dickensian novel “Oliver Twist, or the Parish Boy's Progress.” The three lit buildings in this collection are inspired by those most prominent in the story. This is the fifth of Dickens' works to be added to the village. The accessory set contains principle characters from the novel.
