Christmas Market Booths
Revised 1/15/2021

Bavarian Christmas Markets, which are traditionally held in the months of November and December, are very popular festivals. People travel from all over the world to partake of handmade crafts, cultural food and drinks, along with the frivolity of the event. The Christmas Market Booths subseries was designed by Department 56 Artist Tom Bates. Traditionally, they were introduced with the mid-year introductions. Each design features an LED lit booth and vendor. In 2021, it was decided to retire the entire subseries.

There have been several general accessories designed, since the initial launch, that complement the subseries very well and are often used by collectors. For that reason, they are listed here as well.
