Day Of The Dead
Revised 12/1/2022

For many years, avid Halloween collectors had requested a collection based on the traditions of the holiday celebrated between October 31st and November 2nd known as “Dia de los Muertos” or Day Of The Dead. In 2019, Department 56 responded in grand fashion with a series based on the Mexican holiday which also includes numerous Coordinating Accessories.
