East Harbor
Revised 8/21/2020

The East Harbor subseries is inspired by New York Harbor, one of the largest harbor in the world. New York Harbor is critical to the import and export industries of the country. Renowned for fishing industry and the ferrys that run the coastline, East Harbor is a perfect addition for those collectors who would also like to display items from the American Pride series as well.
