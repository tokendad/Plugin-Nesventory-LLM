Hide-A-Way Hollow- Christmas
Revised 6/17/2020

Hide-A-Way Hollow is a small series of resin building facades that began with a Christmas theme and was part of the giftware line. The collection featured pewter raccoon, squirrel and bunny accessories that were made to coordinate with and fit inside of the buildings. The collection is packaged in decorative white boxes with acrylic sheet fronts. The entire series was introduced in 1988 and retired in 1991.
