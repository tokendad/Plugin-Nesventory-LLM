THE ORIGINAL SNOW VILLAGE - Limited to Year of Production
Revised 9/9/2025

The Original Snow Village, like other village collections, has had several buildings and holiday gift sets introduced that were limited to a specific year of production. The first piece in this category was not announced until after 27 years after the collection was introduced. While the Santa Comes To Town accessory series, the Brite Lites Christmas Parade, and Travels With Ed are each ongoing Limited To Year of series, they are listed separately from this listing.
