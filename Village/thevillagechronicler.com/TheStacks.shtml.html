The Stacks
 Revised 11/15/2025
The Stacks listing consists of the History List for each individual village series (categorized by classification) produced by Department 56. The listing for villages includes each individual lighted building and the coordinating accessories by item number, as well as the year of introduction and retirement for each. Listings for the individual subseries (‘) to the respective villages may be found in The Collections section.

The listings for the Figurine Collections may be found at The Figurines and presently include only the most popularly collected figurines.

We currently have 11,885 items in our database.


Items that have a (Dash and a letter) following the item number are items that are part of a set.

 If you are not sure how to find what you are looking for click on the link below and then click on the "All Products" link, and follow the search instructions on that page.
