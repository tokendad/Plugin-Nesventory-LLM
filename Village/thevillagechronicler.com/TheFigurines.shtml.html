The Figurines
 Revised 11/15/2025
The majority of items under this classification were created by Kristi Jensen-Pierro. Kristi’s principle designs area anchored in the Snowbabies and Snowbunnies collections. Her immense talent in designing figures has been called upon to create the whimsical and lifelike designs of numerous other collections, all of which are considered giftware items.

After Kristi Jensen-Pierro’s retirement in 2004 her designs continued until 2025. Beginning with the 40th anniversary of Snowbabies in 2006, designs were created by Kiri Namtvedt.

We currently have 11,885 items in our database.


Items that have a (Dash and a letter) following the item number are items that are part of a set.
